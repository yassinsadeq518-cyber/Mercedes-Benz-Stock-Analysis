# Mercedes-Benz-Stock-Analysis
# 📉 Financial Analytics: Mercedes-Benz Stock Performance (2020-2024)

## 📌 Project Overview
A financial intelligence dashboard focused on tracking, visualizing, and analyzing the stock market performance of Mercedes-Benz Group AG over a volatile 5-year economic period.

## 📊 Key Insights & Features
* **Price Milestones:** Visualizes yearly Open, High, Low, and Close averages, marking a peak closing average of €69.07 in 2024.
* **Volume Analysis:** Captures a significant downward trend in market trading volume, shifting from 341M transactions in 2020 to 66M in 2024.
* **Volatility Metrics:** Calculated historical price deviations to evaluate stock stability during broader market shifts.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Skills
* **Tool:** Power BI Desktop
* **Financial DAX:** Custom calculations for Moving Averages, Year-over-Year (YoY) growth, and price ranges.
* **Data Visualizations:** Line and Area charts optimized for stock trend interpretations.
